01 Who We Are

VYNEIT is an online t-shirt store operated in India. We sell premium quality t-shirts and related apparel through our website at vyneit.shop.

This Privacy Policy explains how VYNEIT ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects the personal information you ("you", "your", "customer") provide when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

By using our website and placing an order, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

02 Information We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you interact with our website:

Information you give us directly:

  • Full name and email address when you place an order or sign up
  • Shipping address and phone number for delivery purposes
  • Payment information (processed securely — we do not store card details)
  • Messages you send us through our contact form or email
  • Your size preferences and product reviews if submitted

Information collected automatically:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and links clicked on our website
  • IP address and approximate location (country/city level)
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 5)

03 How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders and send order confirmations
  • To arrange delivery of your purchases to your address
  • To communicate order updates, shipping notifications, and tracking info
  • To respond to your questions, complaints, or support requests
  • To send promotional emails and discount offers (only if you opt in)
  • To improve our website, products, and customer experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized transactions
  • To comply with legal obligations under Indian law
Marketing emails: We will only send you marketing emails if you have given us permission. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any email we send you.

04 Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their marketing purposes. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Delivery Partners: We share your name and address with courier/logistics companies (such as Shiprocket, Delhivery, or BlueDart) to deliver your order
  • Payment Processors: Payment information is handled by secure third-party payment gateways (such as Razorpay or PayPal). We do not store your card details
  • Technology Providers: We use Supabase for our database and website hosting services that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority in India
  • Business Transfer: If VYNEIT is acquired or merges with another business, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction

All third parties we work with are required to keep your information secure and use it only for the specific purpose we authorise.

05 Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function, including keeping items in your cart (stored in your browser's localStorage)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language or currency settings

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to shop on our website.

06 Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal requirements
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent to marketing emails at any time
  • Right to Portability: Request that we transfer your data to another service provider
  • Right to Object: Object to how we process your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address provided in Section 11. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

07 Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We use the following measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption on all pages of our website (HTTPS)
  • Secure, encrypted database storage through Supabase
  • Payment processing through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways
  • Access to customer data is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security reviews of our systems and processes
Important: While we take every precaution to protect your data, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

08 Data Retention

We keep your personal information only for as long as necessary:

  • Order Data: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes under Indian law
  • Account Data: Kept for as long as your account is active, plus 2 years after closure
  • Marketing Data: Deleted promptly upon unsubscription
  • Analytics Data: Anonymised and retained for up to 24 months

After the applicable retention period, your data is securely deleted or anonymised.

09 Children's Privacy

VYNEIT is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it from our records.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify you by email if the changes are significant
  • Post a notice on our website homepage for 30 days after a major update

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of our website after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
